About Event Horizon

Our Mission

To illuminate the obscure, demystify the complex, and explore the boundaries of what is known.

The Philosophy

Event Horizon is not just a blog; it is a curation of the bleeding edge. We believe that science is not a static collection of facts, but a dynamic, often messy, and incredibly exciting process of discovery. From the quantum mechanics of negative mass to the macro-engineering of jet engines, and the ethical moralities of modern technology, we aim to bring you stories that challenge your perspective.

The Curator

This space is maintained by an enthusiast of the unknown. Inspired by the deep dives of creators like Veritasium, we summarize and expand upon the most fascinating scientific narratives of our time.
